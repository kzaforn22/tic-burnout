Expected Outcomes (Modeled)

The capstone originally presented hypothetical results. Here, outcomes are framed as expected directions of effect, informed by prior literature and public workforce stress research. This section intentionally avoids claiming empirical findings that were not collected.

Illustrative (not empirical) visualization of expected directions: ↓ emotional exhaustion, ↓ depersonalization, ↑ personal accomplishment.

Implementation success indicators (consulting-ready KPIs)

  • Improved staff perceptions of psychological safety and supervisor support.
  • Reduced reported burnout risk indicators (using validated scales).
  • Downstream operational signals: reduced absenteeism, reduced turnover intentions, improved retention.
