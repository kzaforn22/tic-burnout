TIC × Burnout

Executive Summary

This microsite presents an evidence-informed capstone proposal examining whether trauma-informed care (TIC) training may reduce burnout among juvenile justice professionals. It integrates (1) a structured synthesis of peer-reviewed literature, (2) publicly available corrections workforce datasets used as contextual evidence, and (3) a proposed quasi-experimental evaluation design.

What is on this site

  • Skimmable briefs for leaders and recruiters, with “read more” depth.
  • Conceptual diagrams you can reuse in talks and posters.
  • Transparent scholarship: all external claims are cited; no fabricated results.

How to use it

  • Use the tabs to jump between sections.
  • Use “Evidence Snapshot” to understand the broader workforce context.
  • Use “Methods” to see how the TIC evaluation would be implemented.
