Discussion & Implications

For organizational leaders

  • Risk management: Burnout is a predictable operational risk in trauma-exposed systems.
  • Retention strategy: TIC can be positioned as a workforce sustainability intervention.
  • Quality improvement: Staff well-being supports consistent, relational youth services.

For policy and funding

  • Training investments can be justified through cost-avoidance logic (turnover, overtime, safety incidents).
  • Statewide TIC standards can create implementation consistency across facilities.

For consulting / applied organizational psychology

  • Package TIC as a change program: training + coaching + policy alignment + measurement.
  • Use baseline measurement and periodic audits to prevent “checkbox training.”
  • Link staff outcomes to organizational outcomes (citizenship behavior, counterproductive behaviors, climate).
