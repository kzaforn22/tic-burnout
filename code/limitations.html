Limitations & Future Research

Limitations of the capstone proposal

  • No primary data were collected; results are hypothetical and presented transparently as such.
  • Quasi-experimental grouping introduces selection bias (who gets trained may differ systematically).
  • Regional scope limits generalizability without multi-site replication.

Future research (Ivy-standard next steps)

  • Longitudinal tracking (burnout and retention over time).
  • Randomized or stepped-wedge training rollouts where feasible.
  • Test mediation: does psychological safety/supervision explain the effect of TIC on burnout?
  • Cross-sector replication (healthcare, education, child welfare).
Ethics note: This site does not present fabricated results. If you want real quantitative outputs, the next step is acquiring dataset files and running reproducible analyses.
